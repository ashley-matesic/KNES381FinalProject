The relative initiation of the hip drop to hand drop was calculated for each cycle by subtracting hand drop time from the hip drop time, the same was performed for cessation of hips relative to hands. An average and standard deviation of relative hip drop initiation and cessation were obtained by using the "average" and "stdev" functions in excel.
